What is PINtPOINT?

PINtPOINT is a beer-first pub discovery app that shows you what is actually on tap before you arrive. A live radar ranks nearby venues by what they are pouring right now, filterable by beer style, so every suggestion is already relevant to the kind of pint you want next. Currently tracking 1,091+ venues across 40+ countries, with 3,140+ beers matched to tap lists.

The pitch in one line: most beer apps help you remember what you drank. PINtPOINT helps you decide what to drink next.
