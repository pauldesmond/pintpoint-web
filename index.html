Radar That Points the Way

A live radar sweeps your surroundings and plots nearby venues as blips. Tap any blip and it transforms into a live compass pointing you directly at your chosen pub. Pinch to zoom, 2 fingers to pan.

🔍

Browse, Search & Filter

Fully searchable venue list with photos and ratings. Filter by beer style — West Coast IPA, Bitter, Belgian Ale, and more — so every result is already relevant before you tap.

✨

Discover Hidden Gems

Surface venues you've never visited, automatically. Browse rich venue cards with photos, ratings, and open/closed status. Add anywhere to your PINtPOINT list with a single tap.

🗺️

Create-a-Crawl

Bimble no more. Build a custom pub crawl from venues around you in seconds. Set your stops, max gap, and total distance. Share the route with your group via QR code. Works anywhere in the world.

🔔

Watchlist Alerts

Add a beer to your Watchlist and PINtPOINT notifies you when it's being poured nearby. Perfect for chasing that seasonal release or rare import.

🎨

Built Around You

Customise your radar range, theme and accent colour. Favourite the places worth going back to. A full in-app guide means you'll never miss a feature.
