How to install

  1. Tap Download PINtPOINT.apk above Your browser will warn you the file "may harm your device" — it won’t, but Google shows that message for every non-Play install. Tap Download anyway.
  2. Open the APK from your Downloads Notification bar or Files app → Downloads → tap PINtPOINT.apk.
  3. Allow install from this source Android prompts "For your security, your phone isn’t allowed to install unknown apps from this source." Tap Settings → toggle Allow on for Chrome (or whichever browser). Back out and open the APK again.
  4. Install Tap Install → Done. PINtPOINT is now in your app drawer.
  5. Redeem your license Open PINtPOINT → Settings → Redeem License Key → paste the 32-character key from your email.
Auto-updates: since PINtPOINT isn’t on the Play Store, Android won’t auto-update it. We push UI & feature updates silently over-the-air, but roughly twice a year we’ll release a new APK — we’ll nudge you from inside the app when that happens.

Why side-load?

Google Play requires publishing developer contact info (home address, phone number) on every app’s public listing. That’s a non-starter for a solo developer. Side-loading lets us deliver the app directly without forcing PII disclosure — and saves the 15-30% Play fee, which is why a lifetime license works at £9.99 instead of an ongoing subscription.
