The Firkin Dog-Head

In 1990, David Bruce's twelve original Firkin pubs were printed on a paper leaflet handed out across the chain. Plotted on a modern map, the route between them traces — deliberately — the outline of a dog's head. The pun was Dogbolter, the chain's flagship beer. Seven of the twelve are still pubs under other names. Five are ghost stops: one demolished, four converted.
