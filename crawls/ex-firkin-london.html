Ex-Firkin London

Twenty-two London pubs where David Bruce's original brewpubs and the Firkin Pub Co's chain-era expansion still stand. Some kept the name, most didn't. One kept the brewing. Five walkable chapters cover Central, South, North, West and the three Bruce-era Originals. Plus a Kew wildcard (Flower & Firkin) and a Romford memorial (the Ford & Firkin, now closed).
