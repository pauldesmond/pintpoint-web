Full Feature List

Every Feature

No map. No clutter. Just the tools that help you decide where to drink, what's on, and what's worth the walk.

🔍 Find & Explore 3 features +
Discover

Surface venues you've never visited. Discover searches both Google Places and Foursquare for nearby pubs and bars, showing rich cards with photos, ratings, and live venue details. Add any discovery to your PINtPOINT list with one tap.

🔍
Dual-Source Discovery
Searches both Google Places and Foursquare so venues that one source might miss still surface. Better coverage, fewer gaps.
📍
Configurable Radius
Search within a range that suits you, from a short local wander to a wider destination search.
🟢
Open Now Badges
Discovered venues show whether they're currently open or closed, along with ratings and other useful context where available.
🔗
Find on Untappd
Tap to search for a discovered venue on Untappd and check what they're pouring before adding it.
🍻
Crawl from Discoveries
Found a cluster of interesting places? Build a pub crawl directly from your discovered venues.
Live Radar & Compass

A live radar sweeps your surroundings and plots nearby venues as colour-coded blips. Tap a blip to lock on and the radar becomes a live compass pointing you directly at your chosen pub, with real-time distance and bearing.

Blip Legend
  • Nearby venue
  • Favourited venue
  • Matches style filter
  • Struggling pub
  • At-risk pub
  • Critical pub
  • Ghost pub
🔒
Lock-on Navigation
Tap any blip to lock on. See bearing, distance, and a compass needle that updates as you move and turn.
🔄
Two Display Modes
Switch between nearest venues and all venues within your chosen radius.
🤏
Pinch & Pan
Pinch to zoom your radar range and explore beyond your immediate area.
🗺️
Maps Handoff
When locked on, hand off to Apple Maps or Google Maps for turn-by-turn directions.
Tap Roulette

Can't decide where to go? Tap Roulette picks a nearby venue and sends you straight there — no overthinking, no committee meeting, just a decision made.

📍
Radius Control
Set how far you're willing to travel, from a short walk to a longer wander. Results are filtered to your chosen range.
🎯
Lock & Go
Once a venue is picked, you get the essentials — distance, details, and a one-tap handoff to Maps.
🔄
Spin Again
Not feeling it? Spin again and let fate have another go.
🍺 Beer 2 features +
Beer Alerts

Never miss your favourite beer. Add beers to your watchlist, and PINtPOINT alerts you when they appear at your favourited venues. Matches are based on recent tap-list and check-in data.

📝
Watchlist
Build a list of beers you love. Add as many as you want and remove any you no longer care about.
❤️
Favourited Venue Alerts
Alerts are matched against venues you've saved in PINtPOINT, so notifications stay relevant to the places you actually care about.
🔔
Push Notifications
Get notified when a watched beer is spotted at one of your favourited venues.
📲
Checks on Open
Alerts also run when you open the app, so you can quickly see what's appeared since your last visit.
Beer Can Scan

Point your camera at a beer can or bottle barcode and PINtPOINT will try to identify it using the Open Food Facts database. Recognition works best on beers with registered barcodes, and some labels won't match.

🔍
Instant Recognition
Scan a barcode and PINtPOINT looks it up against Open Food Facts.
🍺
Beer Details
If a match is found, you'll see the beer name and brewery so you can save it or look it up further.
🔔
Add to Watchlist
Spot a beer you love on a shelf? Add it to your watchlist and get alerted when it appears on tap at one of your favourited venues.
📦
Cans & Bottles Only
Beer Can Scan works on packaged cans and bottles with barcodes.
🗺️ Nights Out 2 features +
Create-a-Crawl

Plan a pub crawl in seconds. Set your starting point, number of stops, maximum gap between venues, and total walking distance. PINtPOINT builds a route using a nearest-neighbour algorithm — picking the closest unvisited venue at each step.

📐
Auto-Generated Routes
Set the crawl length and walking constraints. PINtPOINT picks the closest unvisited venue at each step to build your route.
🚶
Walk Times
See estimated walking times between stops, plus total distance and route length.
✅
Progress Tracking
Mark stops as visited and pick up where you left off.
📲
Share with Friends
Generate a QR code so friends can load the same crawl instantly.
Share & QR

Share venues and pub crawl routes with friends using PINtPOINT QR codes. One scan loads the venue or route — no links, no accounts, no faff.

🃏
Tap Trumps
Share any venue as a collectible card with photo, stats, and PINtPOINT branding.
🗺️
Crawl QR Codes
Share an entire pub crawl route with all stops and directions.
📷
Built-in Scanner
Scan PINtPOINT QR codes directly in the app.
🏚️ Pub Culture 2 features +
Ghost Hunter

PINtPOINT doesn't just show what's still open. Toggle Ghost Hunter and your radar transforms — closed and demolished pubs (primarily London) appear as purple blips. Tap any ghost to see what it once looked like, and what replaced it.

📸
Then & Now
See historical photography alongside current imagery where available.
🟣
Purple Blips
Ghost pubs appear as solid purple blips on the radar, with many linked to archive photography and historical notes.
💀
Demolished Mode
Filter to show only the pubs that no longer exist at all.
Troubled Pubs

Some pubs need your support before it's too late. PINtPOINT uses Financial Pressure Index data from ismypubfucked.com to highlight venues under financial strain, from "Feeling It" to "Absolutely F**ked".

FPI Levels
  • Fine
  • Feeling It
  • Struggling
  • F**ked
  • Absolutely F**ked
🟠
Amber Radar Blips
Troubled pubs stand out on the radar so you can spot them instantly.
🧭
Lock-On Warning
Tap a troubled venue and PINtPOINT surfaces its current pressure level clearly.
📊
Venue FPI Card
Venue pages show FPI score, severity label, and rate-change context, credited to ismypubfucked.com and VOA data.
