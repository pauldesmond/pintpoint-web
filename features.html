Radar

A live radar sweeps your surroundings and plots nearby venues as colour-coded blips. Tap a blip to lock on — the radar transforms into a live compass pointing you directly at your chosen pub, with real-time distance and bearing.

🔒 Lock-on Navigation

Tap any blip to lock on. The display shows bearing, distance, and a compass needle that updates as you move and turn.

🔄 Two Display Modes

Nearest venues shows the N closest pubs regardless of distance. All within radius shows everything in your search area.

🤏 Pinch & Pan

Pinch to zoom your radar range. Two-finger drag to pan the view. Lets you explore beyond your immediate area.

🗺️ Maps Handoff

When locked on, tap the maps button for turn-by-turn directions in Apple Maps (iOS) or Google Maps (Android).

🍺

Venues

A fully searchable list of venues with photos, ratings, and recent check-in data. Search by venue name or beer name. Sort by distance or style match. Filter by beer style, rating, ABV, and more.

🔍 Smart Search

Search across venue names and beer names simultaneously. Fuzzy matching means you don't need exact spellings.

📊 Sort Options

Sort by distance to find what's close, or by style match when filtering to find venues with the best selection of your chosen style.

❤️ Favourites

Heart any venue to save it. Favourited venues show as red blips on the radar so you can always spot your regulars.

📸 Rich Cards

Every venue card shows a photo, distance, rating, beer count, and a featured beer pill highlighting what's worth trying.

✨

Discover

Surface venues you've never visited. Discover searches Google Places for pubs and bars near your location, showing rich cards with photos, ratings, and open/closed status. Add any discovery to your PINtPOINT list with one tap.

📍 Configurable Radius

Search within 0.5mi to 15mi. Adjust the radius to explore your neighbourhood or scope out a destination before visiting.

🟢 Open Now Badges

Each discovered venue shows whether it's currently open or closed, plus its Google rating and review count.

🔗 Find on Untappd

Tap to search for any discovered venue on Untappd to check their tap list before adding to PINtPOINT.

🍻 Crawl from Discoveries

Found a cluster of interesting places? Build a pub crawl directly from your discovered venues.

🗺️

Create-a-Crawl

Plan the perfect pub crawl in seconds. Set your starting point, number of stops, maximum gap between venues, and total walking distance. PINtPOINT builds an optimised route using a nearest-next algorithm.

📐 Smart Routing

Set 2-8 stops with configurable max gap and total walk distance. The algorithm finds the best route through nearby venues.

🚶 Walk Times

Real-time walking time estimates between each stop. See total crawl distance and estimated duration at a glance.

✅ Progress Tracking

Mark stops as visited as you go. Resume an active crawl anytime — your progress is saved.

📲 Share with Friends

Generate a QR code for your crawl. Friends scan it to get the full route with all stops and directions.

🎯

Refine Results

Dial in exactly what you're looking for. Filter by beer style, search for a specific beer by name, set minimum ratings, adjust your radius, and toggle special modes — all from one bottom sheet.

🍺 Style Filter

Multi-select from dozens of styles — IPA, Stout, Lager, Sour, Saison, and more. Long-press any style for a description.

🔎 Named Beer Search

Looking for a specific beer? Search by name across all venues to find exactly where it's pouring.

⭐ Rating & ABV

Set minimum rating thresholds and ABV ranges to narrow your results to the beers that match your preferences.

📏 Radius Control

Nine preset radius options from 250 yards to 100 miles. Your selection syncs across the radar and venue list.

🔔

Beer Alerts

Never miss your favourite beer. Add beers to your watchlist, and PINtPOINT will alert you when they appear at any venue in your area. Matched against recent Untappd check-in data.

📝 Watchlist

Build a list of beers you love. Add as many as you want — swipe to remove any you no longer care about.

⚡ Instant Alerts

When you open the app, PINtPOINT checks your saved venues for watchlist matches and shows alerts immediately.

👻

Ghost Hunter

PINtPOINT doesn't just show what's still open. Toggle Ghost Hunter and your radar transforms — 500+ closed and demolished London pubs appear as purple blips. Tap any ghost to see what it once looked like, and what replaced it.

📸 Then & Now

Historical photos alongside current Street View. Tap the venue image to flip between past and present — the same corner, decades apart.

🟣 Purple Blips

Ghost pubs appear as solid purple blips on the radar. 363 with historical photos. 71 demolished entirely. Toggle in Refine Results.

💀 Demolished Mode

Filter to show only the pubs that no longer exist at all — not converted, not closed, just gone. A map of what London lost.

Sharing

Share venues and crawl routes with friends using QR codes. One scan adds the venue or loads the full route — no links, no accounts, no faff.

🃏 Tap Trumps

Share any venue as a collectible card — photo, stats, top beers, and PINtPOINT branding. Send it to WhatsApp, iMessage, or anywhere.

🗺️ Crawl QR Codes

Share an entire pub crawl route as a QR code. Friends join with all stops, distances, and progress tracking intact.

📷 Built-in Scanner

Point your camera at a PINtPOINT QR code to instantly load the venue or route. No third-party app needed.

🎨

Customise

Make PINtPOINT yours. Choose your theme, pick an accent colour, set your preferred units, configure your radar behaviour, and set up Quick Picks for the areas you visit most.

🎨 Themes

Multiple colour themes to suit your taste — Teal, Amber, Purple, Blue and more. Every UI element adapts.

📡 Radar Settings

Choose between Nearest Venues or All Within Radius mode. Set how many blips to show or the radar range.

📌 Quick Picks

Save up to 6 favourite areas (postcodes, neighbourhoods, cities) for instant location switching. Perfect for regulars.

📏 Units

Switch between miles and metres. Distance displays across the entire app update instantly.

📋

Venue Detail

Tap any venue card to see the full picture — tap list, ratings, distance, navigation, sharing, and more.

🍺 Recent Beers

See what's been checked in recently — beer name, style, brewery, and ABV. Sort by rating or ABV.

🧭 Navigate

One tap to open directions in Apple Maps or Google Maps. See distance from your current location.

📲 Share & QR

Share venues as Tap Trumps cards via any messaging app. Share pub crawl routes as QR codes for your group.

🔗 Untappd Link

Jump to the venue's Untappd page to check in, see reviews, and browse the full beer menu.

➕

Add Venue

Found a pub that's not in the database? Add it yourself. Search by name, Untappd URL, or add from your discoveries. PINtPOINT pulls in photos, location data, and recent check-ins automatically.

🔍 Untappd Search

Paste an Untappd venue URL or search by name. PINtPOINT imports recent check-ins, ratings, and venue details.

📸 Auto Photos

Google Places photos are fetched automatically. If the initial fetch fails, photos are lazily loaded when you browse.

🌍 Works Anywhere

Add venues from anywhere in the world. PINtPOINT already has pubs in 37 countries — from Barbados to North Korea. Every venue you add is instantly available to everyone.
