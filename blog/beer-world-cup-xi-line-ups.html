Forty-eight Squads

A few star beers. One line on the team. Grouped A through L.

Group A

🇲🇽Mexico

Corona Extra · Modelo Especial · Pacifico Clara · Negra Modelo · Bohemia

Heritage giant. The world drinks Mexican lager and doesn't always know which one.
🇿🇦South Africa

Castle Lager · Castle Milk Stout · Carling Black Label · Devil's Peak King's Blockhouse IPA

Heritage spine, craft tooth. Castle Lager captains.
🇰🇷South Korea

Cass Fresh · Hite Extra Cold · Terra · Magpie Porter

Macro core. Craft scene quietly arriving from Seoul and Jeju.
🇨🇿Czech Republic

Pilsner Urquell · Budvar (Budweiser Budvar) · Staropramen · Kozel · Bernard

Invented the pilsner. Still owns the format.

Group B

🇨🇦Canada

Molson Canadian · Labatt 50 · Moosehead Lager · Unibroue La Fin du Monde · Steam Whistle Pilsner

Macro spine. Unibroue stealing every man-of-the-match award.
🇧🇦Bosnia & Herzegovina

Sarajevsko · Nektar Pivo · Preminger

Sarajevsko captains. Nektar warms up. Both well thought of.
🇶🇦Qatar

Amstel 0.0 · Heineken 0.0 · Moussy

Alcohol restricted. Squad runs on non-alcoholic imports.
🇨🇭Switzerland

Feldschlösschen · Cardinal · Calanda · BFM Abbaye de Saint Bon-Chien

Macro lagers anchored. BFM does the heavy craft lifting.

Group C

🇧🇷Brazil

Brahma Chopp · Bohemia 1853 · Eisenbahn Pilsen · Wäls Petroleum

Committee adjourned to a botequim and lost quorum.
🇲🇦Morocco

Casablanca Premium · Flag Spéciale · Stork Première · Casablanca Citron

Six honest beers. No apologies.
🇭🇹Haiti

Prestige · Prestige Cosecha Privada

One beer with conviction is still one beer.
🏴󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿Scotland

Tennent's Lager · BrewDog Punk IPA · Harviestoun Schiehallion · Tempest Brave New World

Heineken and Pelforth Ambrée struck — foreign contract pours.

Group D

🇺🇸USA

Sierra Nevada Pale Ale · Pliny the Elder · Two Hearted Ale · Florida Man (Cigar City)

Hop legacy ruling stands.
🇵🇾Paraguay

Pilsen · Pilsen Premium · Brahma Paraguay · Bayern

Pilsen with paperwork is not, technically, a squad.
🇦🇺Australia

Coopers Pale Ale · Stone & Wood Pacific · Mountain Culture Cult IPA · Hawkers West Coast IPA

Modern craft strong, hop legacy stronger.
🇹🇷Turkey

Efes Pilsen · Bomonti Filtresiz · Gara Guzu Black Bear · Efes Dark

Tuborg Türk and Heineken Türkiye contract-struck.

Group E

🇩🇪Germany

Weihenstephaner Hefeweißbier · Augustiner Helles · Schneider Aventinus · Schlenkerla Märzen

Reinheitsgebot keeps being cited as eligibility law. It isn't.
🇨🇼Curaçao

Amstel Bright

Brewed Willemstad since 1960. Older than most countries' independence.
🇨🇮Ivory Coast

Ivoire (Solibra) · Tuborg CI

Pub VAR's longest file. Ivoire native; everything else "pending pending".
🇪🇨Ecuador

Pilsener (1913) · Club Premium · Bandido Andean Pale · Bandido Capuchino Stout

Heritage side wouldn't release captaincy.

Group F

🇳🇱Netherlands

Heineken Original · La Trappe Tripel · Brouwerij 't IJ Zatte · De Molen Hel & Verdoemenis

Westmalle ruled Belgian despite language overlap.
🇯🇵Japan

Asahi Super Dry · Sapporo Premium · Kirin Ichiban · Yebisu · Hitachino Nest White Ale

Macro dry-lager hegemony with Hitachino's craft pedigree on the bench.
🇸🇪Sweden

Norrlands Guld · Omnipollo Magic Number · Stigbergets West Coast IPA · Närke Kaggen Stormaktsporter

Mikkeller ruled Danish. Longest argument of selection night.
🇹🇳Tunisia

Celtia · 33 Export

Celtia carries the country. Honest 2.

Group G

🇧🇪Belgium

Orval · Duvel · Rochefort 10 · Cantillon Gueuze

Abbey called committee to please stop mentioning Westvleteren.
🇪🇬Egypt

Stella (Egyptian) · Sakara Gold · Sakara Premium

Stella Egypt — different Stella, older heritage. Sakara provides the modern foil.
🇮🇷Iran

Delster · Istak · Hofbräu Persia (Tehran) · Argo Tehran (1928–1979, ghost call-up)

Diaspora counts. Non-alcoholic counts. Heritage counts. Volume III still open.
🇳🇿New Zealand

Steinlager Classic · Speight's Gold Medal Ale · Tui East India Pale Ale · Garage Project Hāpi Daze · 8 Wired Hopwired IPA

Heritage trio plus craft fireworks. Garage Project is the wildcard you want.

Group H

🇪🇸Spain

Estrella Damm · Mahou Cinco Estrellas · Alhambra Reserva 1925 · La Pirata Black Block

World thinks Spanish beer is "Estrella with jamón". World is wrong.
🇨🇻Cape Verde

Strela

Strela is the national beer. Honest 1.
🇸🇦Saudi Arabia

Moussy · Holsten Vital · Barbican

Alcohol prohibited. Squad is the non-alcoholic aisle of every Riyadh supermarket.
🇺🇾Uruguay

Pilsen Uruguay · Patricia · Zillertal · Cabesas Bier APA

Macro three-pack, Cabesas adding craft teeth.

Group I

🇫🇷France

Kronenbourg 1664 · Pelforth Brune · Jenlain Ambrée · Brasserie Thiriez Extra

Three regional brewing traditions in a trench coat.
🇸🇳Senegal

Gazelle · Flag Spéciale (Castel)

Gazelle captains. Castel contract pours dominate the rest.
🇮🇶Iraq

Diyala · Farida

Limited domestic scene. Honest 2.
🇳🇴Norway

Ringnes · Mack Bayer · Aass Bock · Nøgne Ø #100 · Lervig Lucky Jack

Heritage macros plus Nøgne Ø and Lervig — both world-class on craft form.

Group J

🇦🇷Argentina

Quilmes Cristal · Patagonia Amber · Antares Honey · Strange Brewing Doble Cordobesa

AB InBev owns roughly half of all available eligibility arguments.
🇩🇿Algeria

Beaufort · Tango

Limited scene. Beaufort and Tango do the work.
🇦🇹Austria

Stiegl Goldbräu · Gösser · Ottakringer · Schremser · Trumer Pils

Quietly excellent. Stiegl and Trumer would walk into most XIs.
🇯🇴Jordan

Carakale · Petra

Carakale is the craft pioneer. Petra is the macro foil.

Group K

🇵🇹Portugal

Super Bock · Sagres · Coral (Madeira) · Letra B

Captaincy unresolved. Dressing room divided. Cristal still complaining.
🇨🇩DR Congo

Primus · Skol (Brassiongo) · Tembo · Doppel Munich

Primus captains. Broad portfolio carries the squad.
🇺🇿Uzbekistan

Sarbast · Pulsar

Limited but present. Sarbast leads.
🇨🇴Colombia

Aguila · Club Colombia · Costeña · Bogotá Beer Company Cajicá Honey Ale

Macro trio with BBC's craft programme behind.

Group L

🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿England

Fuller's London Pride · Greene King IPA · Timothy Taylor's Landlord · Cloudwater DIPA

Boddingtons tabled indefinitely. Contract-brewed Belgium since 2005.
🇭🇷Croatia

Karlovačko · Ožujsko · Pan · Velebitsko

Karlovačko and Ožujsko are the long-running derby. Velebitsko the regional cult pick.
🇬🇭Ghana

Star Beer (Guinness Ghana) · Club Premium · Guinness FES (Ghana) · Star Radler

Honest 8, not 11. Guinness FES eligibility book volume II.
🇵🇦Panama

Balboa · Atlas · La Rana Dorada Pale · La Rana Dorada IPA

Committee asked Pub VAR if one brewery's portfolio counts. Pub VAR went quiet.
