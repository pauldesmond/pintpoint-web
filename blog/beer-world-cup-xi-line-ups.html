Squads as they land

Tap any country to expand the full scouting report. Squads update as Pub VAR rules on disputed picks, the Selection Committee changes its mind, and reader nominations get folded in.

🇧🇷 Brazil ▾
Seed status
Tournament contender — bench depth both ways
Manager's problem
Can't fit modern craft and heritage into one XI
Likely starters
Brahma Chopp, Antarctica Original, Bohemia Pilsen (1853), Eisenbahn Pilsen
Wildcard
Wäls Petroleum — Belo Horizonte imperial-stout heavyweight
Selection controversy
Cervejaria Avós, Dádiva and three other modern-craft picks lost to heritage. Committee adjourned to a botequim and lost quorum.
Pub VAR watching
Heineken Brasil, Stella Artois Brasil and Devassa all locally brewed at Heineken Araraquara — eligibility pending
🇩🇪 Germany ▾
Seed status
Group-of-death seed — 1500 years of brewing tradition
Manager's problem
Several brewers physically attended selection demanding their inclusion
Likely starters
Weihenstephaner Hefeweißbier (captain, 1040 AD), Augustiner Helles, Paulaner Salvator, Schneider Aventinus
Wildcard
Schlenkerla Märzen — Bamberg rauchbier, smoke incarnate
Selection controversy
Beck's, Krombacher, Warsteiner, Veltins and Hofbräu Weisse all caused arguments. Selection meeting abandoned after 17 hours and reconvened at Hofbräuhaus.
Pub VAR watching
The Reinheitsgebot is not technically an eligibility law but Pub VAR keeps quoting it
🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 England ▾
Seed status
Tournament heavyweight — opposite problem to Haiti's
Manager's problem
Too much depth. Cask heritage and modern craft fight for every spot
Likely starters
Fuller's London Pride (captain), Greene King IPA, Adnams Ghost Ship, Timothy Taylor's Landlord
Wildcard
Cloudwater DIPA — Manchester modern craft heavyweight
Selection controversy
Theakston Old Peculier, Hook Norton Old Hooky, Pressure Drop Pale Fire, Kernel Table Beer, Wild Beer Pogo, Burning Sky Plateau and DEYA Steady Rolling Man all caused arguments. Selection Committee declined to take questions.
Pub VAR watching
Boddingtons (Mancunian heritage, contract-brewed in Belgium since the 2005 Strangeways closure) — committee tabled indefinitely. Carling and Stella Artois UK ruled foreign-parent — blocked.
🇳🇱 Netherlands ▾
Seed status
Heritage lager weight (Heineken's gravity well) plus a serious modern craft scene
Manager's problem
How many Heineken sub-brands count as Netherlands picks?
Likely starters
Heineken Original (captain), Amstel Lager, Grolsch Premium Pilsner, La Trappe Tripel
Wildcard
De Molen Hel & Verdoemenis — Bodegraven imperial-stout heavyweight
Selection controversy
Bavaria, Hertog Jan Karakter, Brouwerij Kees! Mosaic Imperial, Oedipus Mannenliefde, Maximus IPA all caused arguments. Brouwerij 't IJ Zatte made the XI; the bench is two pages.
Pub VAR watching
Westmalle Trappist ruled BELGIAN despite the language overlap — blocked. Pub VAR also queried whether Heineken (global megabrand) still counts as Dutch — ruled YES on Amsterdam HQ.
🇸🇪 Sweden ▾
Seed status
Heritage lager AND world-class modern craft — rare combination
Manager's problem
Omnipollo and Stigbergets give the modern bench teeth few teams can match — finding XI room around the heritage names
Likely starters
Norrlands Guld (captain), Spendrups Pilsner, Omnipollo Magic Number, Stigbergets West Coast IPA
Wildcard
Närke Kaggen Stormaktsporter — cult barrel-aged imperial porter
Selection controversy
Dugges Idjit, Beerbliotek Wonderboy and Stigbergets Ghost in the Machine all caused arguments. Mikkeller (Danish, ineligible despite Swedish operations) was the longest argument of all.
Pub VAR watching
Carlsberg Sverige ruled ineligible — Danish parent, contract brewed
🇺🇸 USA ▾
Seed status
Tournament favourite. Birthplace of the West Coast IPA.
Manager's problem
USA selection meeting abandoned after 14 hours
Likely starters
Sierra Nevada Pale Ale, Stone IPA, Russian River Pliny the Elder, Bell's Two Hearted Ale
Wildcard
Florida Man (Cigar City) — listed as impact substitute after being asked to remove the shark tooth necklace before entering play
Selection controversy
Oberon Ale, Lagunitas IPA, Sam Adams Boston Lager, Budweiser, PBR, Coors Banquet and Goose Island Bourbon County all remain under review
Played
USA 2-0 Australia on the pitch · USA on (PINt)POINTS in the pub
🇦🇺 Australia ▾
Seed status
Coopers heritage + serious modern craft bench
Manager's problem
Australia are arguing over heritage versus hop-forward modernity
Likely starters
Coopers Pale Ale, Coopers Sparkling Ale, Stone & Wood Pacific Ale, Hawkers West Coast IPA
Wildcard
Mountain Culture Cult IPA — NSW modern craft breakthrough
Selection controversy
Range, Feral Biggie Juice, Mountain Goat, Tooheys New, James Boag and XXXX Gold all caused arguments. The committee ruled the modern IPA scene strong but not enough.
Played
USA 2-0 Australia on the pitch · USA on (PINt)POINTS in the pub
🏴󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿 Scotland ▾
Seed status
Lager heritage + modern craft + stout depth
Manager's problem
Several breweries already demanding a recount
Likely starters
Tennent's Lager, BrewDog Punk IPA, Innis & Gunn Original, Harviestoun Schiehallion
Wildcard
Tempest Brave New World — late Selection Committee Tweedbank pick
Selection controversy
Williams Bros Joker IPA and Orkney Dark Island made the XI; the bench is full of arguments
Played
Scotland 0-1 Morocco on the pitch · Scotland on (PINt)POINTS — Pub VAR ruled Heineken and Pelforth Ambrée ineligible as foreign-brewed contract pours
🇹🇷 Turkey ▾
Seed status
Efes heritage anchored + small but real craft scene with teeth
Manager's problem
Carlsberg/Heineken contract brewing is the eligibility minefield
Likely starters
Efes Pilsen (captain), Bomonti Filtresiz, Pablos Brewing IPA, Gara Guzu Black Bear
Wildcard
Efes Dark — old-school dark-lager pick
Selection controversy
Marmara Beer, Mojo IPA and Pablos Reserve all caused arguments
Played
Turkey 0-1 Paraguay on the pitch · Turkey on (PINt)POINTS — Tuborg Türk and Heineken Türkiye ruled ineligible
🇪🇨 Ecuador ▾
Seed status
Pilsener heritage anchored, real modern craft depth via Quito
Manager's problem
50/50 split between old and new — finding XI room for both
Likely starters
Pilsener (Cervecería Nacional, captain since 1913), Club Premium, Bandido Brewing Quito Cream Ale, Sabai Sabai Andean Pale Ale
Wildcard
Bandido Brewing Capuchino Stout — coffee-stout pick with an award shelf
Selection controversy
Selection Committee gave Bandido captaincy-bench consideration; the heritage side wasn't having it
Pub VAR watching
Quilmes Ecuador (Argentine parent), Heineken Ecuador (contract), Corona Ecuador (Mexican parent) — all under review
🇵🇦 Panama ▾
Seed status
Heritage-lager spine backed by La Rana Dorada's modern taprooms
Manager's problem
La Rana Dorada essentially carries the modern bench solo
Likely starters
Balboa (captain), Atlas, Soberana, La Rana Dorada Pale Ale
Wildcard
La Rana Dorada IPA — craft IPA flagship
Selection controversy
Committee asked Pub VAR if "one brewery's portfolio" can constitute a squad. Pub VAR went quiet.
Pub VAR watching
Heineken Panama, Corona Panamá (Mexican parent), Tecate Panama — all under review
🇵🇾 Paraguay ▾
Seed status
Pilsen carries the country
Manager's problem
Pilsen, Pilsen Premium, Pilsen Ice and Pilsen-adjacent Munich Lager is one beer with paperwork, not a squad
Likely starters
Pilsen (heritage captain), Pilsen Premium, Brahma Paraguay, Sajonia Bier Schwarz
Wildcard
Bayern (Paraguay) — German-style local lager
Selection controversy
Selection Committee gives Pilsen its due rather than padding with rumour. Bench respectfully short.
Played
Turkey 0-1 Paraguay on the pitch · Turkey on (PINt)POINTS in the pub
🇲🇦 Morocco ▾
Seed status
Desert-heat pilsner core + Casablanca citrus reinforcement
Manager's problem
Pub VAR reviewing two locally-brewed internationals for eligibility
Likely starters
Casablanca Premium Lager, Flag Spéciale, Stork Première, Castel Beer
Wildcard
Casablanca Citron — shandy-style beachside refresher
Selection controversy
Bavaroise made the XI; the squad is honestly six deep
Played
Scotland 0-1 Morocco on the pitch · Scotland on (PINt)POINTS — Heineken and Pelforth Ambrée ruled ineligible as foreign-contract pours
🇬🇭 Ghana ▾
Seed status
Star Beer heritage anchored, established lager bench, Pub VAR's eligibility book on Guinness FES Ghana reaches volume II
Manager's problem
Modern craft bench is thin; honest 8 not 11
Likely starters
Star Beer (captain, Guinness Ghana brewery), Club Premium Lager, Guinness Foreign Extra Stout (Ghana), Castle Milk Stout
Wildcard
Star Radler — citrus shandy variant
Selection controversy
Selection Committee respected the thin squad rather than inventing depth from rumour
Pub VAR watching
Guinness FES (Ghana) — brewed locally by Guinness Ghana since 1971, eligibility ruling pending. Heineken Ghana and Stone Lager Premium also under review.
🇨🇮 Ivory Coast ▾
Seed status
Ivoire is genuine heritage. The rest of the squad is foreign-contract.
Manager's problem
Pub VAR's Ivory Coast eligibility file is the longest of any country so far
Likely starters
Ivoire (captain, Solibra), Bock CI, Castel Côte d'Ivoire, Brassivoire
Wildcard
Tuborg CI — Carlsberg under contract, eligibility minefield
Selection controversy
Ivoire ruled unambiguously native. Everything else is "review pending pending."
Pub VAR watching
Most "Ivorian" beers are foreign brands brewed under contract by Brassivoire (SAB-Miller) or Solibra (Castel)
🇨🇼 Curaçao ▾
Seed status
One beer with heritage. The Selection Committee respects this.
Manager's problem
The squad is, honestly, one beer
Likely starters
Amstel Bright — locally brewed at Amstel Brewery Willemstad since 1960. The only Amstel beer brewed outside the Netherlands.
Wildcard
Polar Pilsen — Venezuelan but ubiquitous; eligibility ruled NO
Selection controversy
Bench respectfully thin. Pub VAR opens another Amstel Bright.
Pub VAR watching
Amstel Bright's locally-brewed-since-1960 status: ruled YES, older than most countries' independence
🇭🇹 Haiti ▾
Seed status
The committee found six honest beers, three eligibility hearings, and no reason to pretend otherwise
Manager's problem
Prestige carries the entire squad
Likely starters
Prestige — captain, vice-captain, brewer, kit-man, manager and dog. The only beer.
Wildcard
Prestige Cosecha Privada — anniversary special-edition reinforcement
Selection controversy
Selection Committee respectfully declined to invent an 8-beer bench from craft rumour
Played
Brazil 3-0 Haiti on the pitch · Brazil on (PINt)POINTS — Prestige carried the squad with belief, but Brazil's bench was deeper both ways
