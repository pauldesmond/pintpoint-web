World Cup host geography is messy though: "Dallas" might mean Arlington, "San Francisco" might mean Santa Clara, "New York / New Jersey" might mean East Rutherford. The fixture-list name and the ground-truth address are not always the same thing — local knowledge keeps the map sharp.

What counts as useful?

For the Stadium Beer Map, we kept places a travelling fan could realistically use before or after a match. The aim was simple — help fans find somewhere decent near the ground. Not just "a bar somewhere in the city". Not a generic search result. Not a famous place that looks good on a list but makes no sense on matchday.

  • walk, transit or short ride
  • beer-relevant
  • better than the nearest "bar"
  • not a city-centre fake
  • scouted, not searched

That last one matters. The map isn't finished. The scouting file is open — and the tournament is the test.

Help complete the map
