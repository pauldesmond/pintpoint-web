Richmond, London — Now Live on PINtPOINT

PINtPOINT tracked zero venues in Richmond-upon-Thames as recently as this morning. As of this afternoon, fifteen are live with tap lists — a gap filled in a single pass.

Richmond is a curious case for UK beer coverage. It's a craft-heavy corner of London (Tap Tavern alone runs a 20-tap rotating keg wall, Richmond Vault Beer Cellar stocks 100+ bottles, Brewstation opened a brand-new taproom in June 2024), but its postcode ambiguity — TW9/TW10 straddles the Greater London / Surrey psychological boundary — means a lot of "London beer map" coverage skips it. We'd rather not skip it.

Now tracked with live tap lists

Specialist beer venues
  • Tap Tavern — Princes St, TW9 1ED. 20 rotating keg/cask lines; Kernel, Siren, Verdant, Cloudwater, Buxton in regular rotation.
  • Richmond Vault Beer Cellar — 5 Hill St, TW9 1SX. Basement Georgian space, 100+ craft bottles.
  • Brewstation Tap Room — Petersham Rd, TW10 6UW. Askrigg (Yorkshire) brewery taproom, opened June 2024.
  • The Mitre — 20 St Mary's Grove, TW9 1UY. 14 handpumps, Cask Marque accredited.
Craft-forward pubs
  • The Prince's Head — 28 The Green, TW9 1LX.
  • The Botanist on the Green — 3-5 Kew Green, TW9 3AA. Own micro-brewery on-site.
  • The Roebuck — 130 Richmond Hill, TW10 6RN. Richmond's oldest pub (1717), hilltop.
  • The Old Ship — 3 King St, TW9 1ND. Young's house, 1735.
  • Tap on the Line — Kew Gardens Station, TW9 3PZ. Station-platform pub, 6 cask + 5 craft.
  • The Greyhound — 82 Kew Green, TW9 3AP.
  • Coach & Horses Kew — 8 Kew Green, TW9 3BH.
Riverside + traditional
  • The White Cross — Riverside, TW9 1TH.
  • Watermans — 10-12 Water Lane, TW9 1TJ.
  • The Cricketers — 24 The Green, TW9 1LX.
  • The Rose & Crown — 79 Kew Green, TW9 3AH.

What's next for the area

Eight of the fifteen have Untappd venue IDs matched and verified (which means the overnight tap-list scraper will start pulling live beer data tonight). The remaining seven — smaller or more traditional pubs that don't track their taps on Untappd — will surface on the radar as location-only entries until their publicans either start a check-in trail or tell us otherwise.

If you drink in Richmond and we've missed a venue worth listing — or if you run one and want to surface a tap list — hello@pintpoint.co.uk.
