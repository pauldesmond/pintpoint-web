Beer Day Britain: Raise a Glass at a Proper Pub

Today is Beer Day Britain — the annual 15 June excuse to celebrate beer, pubs, brewers, bars, taprooms, and the very British skill of choosing exactly the right pint in exactly the right pub.

At 7pm, people across the country are invited to join the official Cheers to Beer toast. We are strongly in favour of this sort of thing.

If you haven't already picked a pub for it, that's what Pintpoint is for. Open the app, find a proper one near you, and get there in time for 7pm. Bonus points for somewhere with a good tap list, a decent crowd, and tables worth leaning on.

Raise a glass at 7pm. Support a proper pub. Tomorrow we go back to arguing about the right pint for the right pub. Today we just drink one.
