Spot the Mat

A batch of Pintpoint beermats is out in the wild. Find one — there's something in it for you.

[ hero image — Blue Maid snifter on the mat, pending DM permission ]

If you see a Pintpoint beermat in the wild, take a photo.

Not a paid listing. Not a sponsored campaign. Not some laminated nonsense from head office. Just a little square of card that's quietly made its way to a table somewhere, because it felt like the kind of place Pintpoint exists for.

We've had a batch of 750 of these things printed, and we're starting to leave them in the places we rate: proper pubs, good beer bars, taprooms, backstreet finds, railway-arch gems, the occasional suspiciously excellent local that deserves more attention. No certificates. No partnerships. No clipboards. Just mats, on tables, in pubs.

Which makes them a small game.

How it works

Spot a Pintpoint mat in a pub. Take a photo. Send us the photo and the name of the pub, and you're in the draw.

Each month, one spotter wins a £25 Pintpoint pub fund — enough for a couple of pints somewhere good. We'll also send a small stack of mats to the pub they nominate, if they want them.

The campaign spreads through the people playing it. The more mats out there, the more there are to spot. The more there are to spot, the more pubs end up with one on the table.

That's the whole loop. You find one in the wild, you put one in the wild.

What counts

Any photo of a Pintpoint mat in a real pub. On a table is best — but on a bar, in a hand, under a glass, propped against a tap badge all count. We're not pedants about composition. We're just looking for proof the mat made it there.

One entry per pub per person. No need to grade the pub or write a review — the photo and the pub name are the whole entry.

What you get

One monthly winner. The prize: a £25 Pintpoint pub fund — enough for a couple of pints somewhere good — and, if you want them, a small stack of mats sent to the pub you nominate. Your pints. Their mats. The next round of spotting starts from their tables.

The point isn't the merch. It's putting another pub on the map.

How to enter

Email a photo and the pub name to hello@pintpoint.co.uk, or tag @pintpoint_app on Instagram with the photo and venue.

That's it. No form. No fields. No "preferred contact method" dropdown.

Monthly draw. Winner announced on socials. £25 Pintpoint pub fund + optional mats for the nominated pub. We've got 730-ish left.

Want some for your pub?

If you run a pub and you'd like a stack of mats on the tables, drop us a line at hello@pintpoint.co.uk. We post them out. There's no scheme, no contract, no logo we want next to ours. The mats are just mats.

The mission is simple. Help people find better pubs. And apparently, it now starts with a beermat.
