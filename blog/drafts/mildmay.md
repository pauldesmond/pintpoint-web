## Flagship blog post — outline

**Title:** Mildmay 2026: Where to Drink Britain's Most Endangered Beer Style
**Slug:** `mildmay-2026-where-to-drink-uk-mild.html`
**Target publish:** Thu 14 May 2026 (3 days after Whippet opens)
**Word count:** 1,200–1,500
**Category:** Essay / Reference

### Spine
Mild was 70% of UK draught beer in 1950. It's <1% today. CAMRA's Mild Month is the annual push to keep it from disappearing entirely. The piece names the breweries reviving it, the pubs pouring it, and the historical reasons it nearly died.

### Sections
1. **The collapse** (~250 words) — From 70% to <1% in 60 years. Why: changing taste, the rise of lager and bitter, brewery consolidation killing regional styles. Mild was the working-class session beer; the working class shifted to keg lager.
2. **What mild actually is** (~200 words) — Style definition. Dark mild vs pale mild. Sessionable strength (3–3.5%). The misconception that "mild = sweet"; in reality it's malt-forward, low-hop, often nutty, can be roasty. Distinct from porter or stout in body and finish.
3. **The revivalists** (~350 words) — Name specific breweries currently brewing mild as a deliberate act: Rivington Brewing Co (Manchester), Bristol Beer Factory (Bristol Mild), Sambrook's (Sambrook's Brunel Mild — actually historic), Five Points (XX Mild), Holden's Black Country Mild (still going), Bathams (still going), Banks's. Differentiate the "still going" stalwarts from the deliberate revivalists.
4. **Where to drink it this May** (~200 words) — PINtPOINT's style filter named pubs currently pouring a mild. Geographic spread. Note: the data is dynamic — what's pouring on the day you tap depends on the day; the freshness dots tell you how recently each line was confirmed.
5. **The cask question** (~150 words) — Mild lives almost entirely on cask. Brief link back to the Sparkler piece (sparklered serve was developed in regions where mild was the dominant pour). Foreshadow the Whippet piece (and link if Whippet is pouring a mild on opening — TBD).
6. **Closing** (~100 words) — Mild Month is partly about preventing extinction. Drink one this month — at one of the named venues, or any pub that's keeping the style alive. Tap the style filter on PINtPOINT to find the closest one.
